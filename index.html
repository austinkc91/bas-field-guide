Sample: Troubleshooting Flowchart

SPACE TOO HOT?
Is the system in Occupied mode?
  NO → Check schedule. Override to Occupied.

Is the thermostat reading correctly?
  Compare to handheld thermometer. Off by >2°F → Recalibrate.

Is the VAV box calling for cooling?
  YES → Is the damper responding to command?
    NO → Check actuator: power? linkage? stroke?
    YES → Is airflow at maximum?
      YES → Is discharge air cold enough?
        NO → AHU issue. Check CHW valve and coil DP.
